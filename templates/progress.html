Activity ≠ progress: impact-weighted timeline

A changelog measures velocity, not scientific advancement — twenty typo fixes and one new computational primitive should not count equally. Each entry is auto-classified into an impact level and weighted. The bars below show each day's composition (segment = impact level); the gold line is the day's weighted score. Where a tall bar carries a low line, that day was busy but low-impact.
